About Me

A simple introduction:

Hello there! My name is Miguel, and I'm a passionate data scientist who is fascinated by the power of AI. The data explosion that is affecting the world has pushed me to explore this path, and I wish to be a part of this exciting movement. Python programming, deep learning, plus learning and researching ability are among my biggest strengths. My proactive approach and ambition, however, I believe, are even more important than my technical skills.

What I'm proud of, what my goals are:

Some of my proudest achievements to date include obtaining the prize for Best Presentation of the Day at Euro S&P 2022, keeping a GPA of 3.6 or higher in all of my degrees, and demonstrating hard-work through internships and scholarships since I was 19. I'm also happy to have also been successful in my personal life. I've lived in 4 different cities, keeping great friends in all and combining personal and professional life in balance. In terms of professional objectives, my goal is to find a challenging job in data science and make an effect at the organization where I work. I don't want to be an average employee; I want my work to leave a lasting impact.

What my hobbies are:

I enjoy listening to music, playing soccer or walk the city when I'm not working or studying. I'm a Spotify addict who has reached 75k yearly minutes for three years in a row, and I've been playing soccer since I was five years old. I've also served as team captain for three years.

What you will like about me:

My optimistic attitude, motivation, and desire to excel distinguish me from others in my field. I feel that having a can-do attitude is essential for success, and I am always motivated to learn new things and face new obstacles. I stay current and knowledgable in my profession by reading a variety of materials such as academic papers, news pieces, and AI newsletters. In addition, finishing my master's degree has provided me with the opportunity to learn cutting-edge technology that I can apply to my profession.
